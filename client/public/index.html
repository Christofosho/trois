Trois

Instructions

The Main Goal

In Trois, you will determine whether there are three cards in play that form a set.

You will judge the cards on 4 traits.

For each trait, the 3 cards must be all the same or they must be all different.

The Traits

SHAPE:
Triangle, Rectangle, Oval
COLOUR:
Blue, Orange, Purple
COUNT:
One, Two, Three
FILL:
Hollow, Shaded, Solid

Example 1

The following three cards are a set.

Matching:

Colour -> Blue

Count -> One

Fill -> Hollow

Distinct:

Shape -> All different

Example 2

The following three cards are a set.

Matching:

Colour -> Blue

Distinct:

Fill -> Hollow, Shaded, Solid

Count -> One, Two, and Three

Shape -> Triangle, Square, Circle

Example 3

The following three cards are NOT a set.

The Fill on each of the three cards is not distinct or matching. All three cards must be distinct or matching on all four traits in order to be accepted as a match.
